About Becky Villaseñor

Becky Villaseñor joined CLM Mortgage as a Loan Officer in 2015. Since then she has been a top producing lender for the company, and has been ranked by the San Antonio Business Journal based on closed loan volume. In 2019-2020 she earned CLM Mortgage’s prestigious Presidents Club Award based on sales volume.

Becky has an extensive finance background spanning over 19 years. She started her financial career at a local registered investment advisory firm and then served as a licensed public finance investment banker.

Throughout her career, Becky's focus has always been on client service and meeting the financial needs of her customers. She will work hard to ensure that the loan process is quick, uncomplicated, and as effortless as possible for her customers.

Becky is licensed by the Texas Department of Savings & Mortgage Lending, NMLS #1388884. She graduated from the University of Texas in Austin and in her spare time, she enjoys keeping busy with her family and friends.
